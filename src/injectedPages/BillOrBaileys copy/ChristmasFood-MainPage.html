“WOULD YOU LIKE THE BILL OR A BAILEYS?”

One simple question, with a massive opportunity to increase spend per head.

Two thirds of customers would treat themselves to a Baileys if they were offered one. Making sure your staff are trained and confident in asking this one simple question at the end of the meal could make the difference between a good night and a great one.

FOUR SIMPLE STEPS TO SERVING GREAT COCKTAILS

Ask the question

“Would you like the bill or a Baileys?” prompts customers to extend their visit and their spend. Research shows 50% are likely to order a post-meal drink or dessert.

Use the menu

Whether it’s with a hot drink, over a dessert or in a cocktail, give your customers the Baileys option – your menu is the perfect place to do it.

Bring the theatre

Using miniature milk bottles to serve Baileys on the side of a dessert or hot drink creates an eye-catching ritual that other diners will envy. +16% sales uplift in trial outlets with this activation.

Offer Baileys Almande

Satisfy the demand for vegan and dairy-free options with a delicious, light-tasting, alternative.
