The diageo drinks report 2019

Welcome to the Diageo Drinks Report 2019 – an introductory report providing insight into the alcoholic beverages category in the UK and beyond.

get the report
Within this report we explore the global trends that have shaped consumer buying behaviour over the last 10 years and look forward to future opportunities and initiatives to create differentiated customer experiences and drive sales in your supermarket, pub, bar or restaurant.
The four key areas

Balanced choices

Stocking the right products to fit with all lifestyles.

Make the moment

Creating moments worth sharing with friends, fans and followers.

Exploration

Providing customers with new ways to discover interesting flavours and experiences.

Celebration

Taking advantage of the unique role that alcoholic beverages play in seasonal events and celebrations.

Transforming drink experiences
Our mission is to transform drinks experiences, and fuel the exciting drinks category, everyday and everywhere.
