Apple 'n' Ginger Captain

Apple 'n' Ginger Captain

Dive into a delicious blend of Captain Morgan, ginger ale and a good slug of apple juice.

Drink Style

Fruity & Fresh | Sweet & Tasty

Season/Occasion

Autumn | Winter | Halloween

Alcohol content

1.75 / 14g

Download recipe card

  • 50ml Baileys Original

  • 20ml espresso

  • 135ml milk

  • Cinnamon stick

  • Nutmeg

  • Whipped cream

  1. Pour 250ml of steaming milk into a disposable cup

  2. Add one espresso shot

  3. Pour in 50ml of Baileys

  4. Add nutmeg, whipped cream and cinnamon stick to serve

top tips

Mint is a very perishable ingredient to keep in a bar. To avoid having to order fresh mint in every day wrap your mint in a damp cloth and store it in a sealed container between uses. Before your shift pick the mint leaves from the stems and store the top of the sprigs to use as a garnish, this will make Mojitos quicker to prepare as you won’t need to pick the leaves from the stems and separate the stem for a garnish for each individual mojito ordeed.
