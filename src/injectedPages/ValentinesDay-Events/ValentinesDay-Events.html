THREE PROFIT-BOOSTING VALENTINE’S EVENTS

Inspired by the mystical love potions

Your top bartender will be bursting with ideas for amazing love themed cocktails. So start selling tickets in pairs to a romantically themed mixology class and have your ingredients at the ready. The perfect pre-dinner event for starcrossed lovers!

PORTRAIT ARTISTS SAY IT WITH A PICTURE

A simple way to create a unique talking point and a romantic momento. Set up a talented local artist in a corner near your bar, sketching quick portraits and fun caricatures of your guests. It’s instantly shareable, extends dwell time and gets everyone talking.

LIVE MUSIC IS THE FOOD OF LOVE

A live musician playing in your venue adds a whole new dimension to the atmosphere and the guest experience. Getting the tone right is vital, so think saxophone, soft jazz and background ballads. More importantly, start advertising now. It will be a key selling point and help you stand out from the crowd.

MORE THAN JUST A FLING

The 14th falling on a Friday gives you the opportunity to extend the Valentine's theme before and after to really maximise the occasion. Curate a Thursday to Sunday programme with these extra suggestions…

  1. Speed Dating: Develop three mixed drink serves, easy to prepare, crowd pleasing cocktails serves, positioning them as the perfect accompaniment to a speed dating event.

  2. Perfect pairing: Put a new spin on your cocktail offering by designing a menu of 12 drinks perfectly attuned to your customers’ star signs.

  3. Valentine’s Day Quiz Night: Run a romantically themed Quiz Night, designing your rounds to suit the occasion – there’s one ready to roll and free to download below. Alternatively, the Mr & Mrs format is easy to recreate and a fun way to test how well the couples in your venue really know each other.
