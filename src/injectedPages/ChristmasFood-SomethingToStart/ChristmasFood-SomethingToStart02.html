Tanqueray Sevilla & Tonic

Relaxed dining

A fruity and zesty gin with the uniquely bittersweet flavour of Seville oranges.

  • 50ml Tanqueray Flor de Sevilla

  • 150ml tonic water

  1. Fill a copa glass with ice

  2. Pour 50ml Tanqueray Flor de Sevilla and top up the glass with tonic

  3. Garnish with a wedge of orange and stick of cinnamon

Johnnie & Ginger

Relaxed dining

An effervescent mix, of Johnnie Walker Gold Label Reserve and Champagne carefully combined with apple, aromatic fresh mint and ginger.

  • 30ml Johnnie Walker Gold Label Reserve

  • 60ml clear apple juice

  • 30ml Champagne

  1. Mix all ingredients together in a Highball glass and serve with garnish.
