Soundry provides music tailored for businesses. Music enables customers to relax and enjoy themselves – they can spend longer with you and spend more. Soundry has made the setup simple, so you can get back to business.

HOW DOES IT WORK?

Soundry curates tracks into playlists for your business ready to be played directly from your computer, iPad, iPhone or Sonos speakers.

WHY DO I NEED SOUNDRY?

When you play music in your venue, you have to pay for the music streaming service (if you use one) as well as to collecting societies. With Soundry you only have to pay €16.99 a month and avoid paying fees to collecting societies for playing the music.

HOW DOES SOUNDRY KNOW HOW TO PERSONALISE THE PLAYLIST?

From your signup Soundry knows which country you have signed up from. For the playlist selection, you simply select from your desired category like ‘type of business’ or ‘energy level’.

HOW DO YOU OPT OUT AFTER THE ONE MONTH ENDS?

You need to sign up with a credit card. After the extended free period, payment will start automatically. If you want to cancel, which you can do at any time even after payment has started, simply login on the web and cancel the subscription under ‘settings.
