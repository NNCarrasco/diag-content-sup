SIX NATIONS –MAKE AN EVENT OF IT

Customers are already getting excited about the 2020 Guinness Six Nations. It’s a huge fixture in the rugby calendar so make an event of it and getmake the most of thisprofit increasing opportunity.

We’ll help you set up events that’ll make the most of the buzz!

Rugby is a point of passion for 15 million drinkers across GBand ROI

Beer and rugby go together. Rugby is proven to drive up sales of beer by 3.4% per outlet during the 2019 Six Nations

Guinness has a long association with rugby, dating back to 1942 when the Guinness works’ rugby club was founded

The Six Nations starts on Saturday 1stFebruary 2020 with Ireland hostingthe second match of the day in Dublin. Fixtures goall the way through to 14thMarch so there are plenty of opportunities to host events.

DECORATIONS FOR THE SIX NATIONS

Guinness is offering activation materials including posters with the ‘Match Made And Settled’ campaign. This will be supported so you get everything lookingfullybranded and official. Pop down to your local rugby club o see if they have any balls or other paraphernaliayou could use to decorate. Advertise the fixtures and include the women’s rugby listings.

PRE-BOOKING

Offer the chance for customers to book. It gives you a guaranteed audience and you can pre-plan your stock. Why not give premium seating closest to the screen(s)? Consider a pre-paid deal with food and drinks so you can really increase yourrevenue.

STOCK UP

Pre-booking gives you the chance to know how much stock you might need. Make sure you consider increased consumption on match days as drinkers bed in for the event–and don’t forget our non-drinking drinkers with a good no and low selection.

LET’S GET QUIZZY

Encourage folk to take their places for the game and to make a night of itwithour rugby quiz sheet. You could always try a little competition too, maybe a rafflein aid of your local club. They might have a signed shirt for you to give as a prize.
