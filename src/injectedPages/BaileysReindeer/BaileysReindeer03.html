  • 50ml Baileys Original Irish Cream

  • 25ml Cold milk

  • Whipped Cream

  • Sprinkles

  • Ice

  • Chocolate Reindeer

  1. Pour Baileys Original Irish cream and cold milk in a cocktail shaker

  2. Add ice and shake for 15 seconds

  3. Strain into a Chocolate vessel

  4. Top it off with whipped cream and chocolate sprinkles
