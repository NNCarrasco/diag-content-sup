FIVE WAYS TO MAKE THIS CHRISTMAS A COMMERCIAL CRACKER

Mark Moriarty, Diageo’s Head Chef in Residence, brings you his top tips for keeping the Christmas tills ringing this year.

The festive period is a brilliant time for creating quality memories with friends and loved ones, and for those of us working in busy bars and restaurants this season also has the potential to be the most profitable time of the year. A win-win in my eyes!

Mark Moriarty

Diageo’s Head Chef in Residence

Read on to find out my five top tips to help you squeeze every last drop out of the ho-ho-holiday season…

1. Think beyond wine and beer

By incorporating a cocktail on the set menu as a ‘Something to Start’ or ‘Something to Finish’, you’re offering new experiences that customers might not have considered.

2. Make your cocktails hot and spicy

New flavour experiences can quickly thaw the chill of a cold night, so serve on arrival or post-dessert to encourage dwell time.

3. Two aperitif and digestif options are enough

It’s easy to overload your guests – and your team – with too much choice, so keep things simple.

4. Twist-up the classics

For example, a Gordon’s Hot Pink blends the crisp, fruity taste of Gordon’s Pink with cinnamon-infused warm raspberry water, as an alternative to a standard tonic.

5. Team up for dessert

Dessert cocktails give your bar and kitchen the chance to collaborate on wonderful serves that look great, taste amazing, and demand to be shared on social.
