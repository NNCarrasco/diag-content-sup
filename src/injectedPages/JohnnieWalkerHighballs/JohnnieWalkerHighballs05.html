1820’s

In 1820, single malts are wildly inconsistent. An enterprising young Johnnie Walker sets out to find a consistent, unique product. His pioneering spirit passes through the generations.

1920’s

The square bottle, slanted label and iconic Striding Man logo are introduced. By 1920, Johnnie Walker reaches 120 countries to become one of the world’s first truly global brands.

Today

Today the passion is alive and kicking. Master blenders and specialists rely on centuries of expertise to create whiskies you simply can’t find anywhere else.
