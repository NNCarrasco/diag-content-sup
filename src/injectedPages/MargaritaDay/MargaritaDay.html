Host Margarita Day in your venue in honour of the international celebration on Saturday 22nd February. Get ready to create some Mexican magic with one of the world's most loved cocktails.

FOLLOW OUR STEP-BY-STEP GUIDE TO MAKING THE PERFECT MARGARITA

STEP 1

Gather the ingredients – Don Julio Blanco tequila, triple sec, gomme syrup, lime juice and a lime wheel to decorate.

STEP 2

Squeeze a shallow coating of lime juice around the rim of a cocktail glass.

STEP 3

Dip the glass into salt.

STEP 4

Put ice into a long glass and add the liquids: 50ml Don Julio tequila, 12½ ml triple sec, 12½ ml gomme syrup, 50ml lime juice.

STEP 5

Shake it to make it!

STEP 6

Pour through a strainer; affix the lime wheel to the side of the glass and serve.

IT'S FIESTA TIME

Host the best happy hour in town to celebrate Margarita Day. How about a 2 for 1 or a free Don Julio Margarita for everyone dining before 6pm?
Encourage group bookings and pair margaritas with a margarita pizza or tacos for a special one day only price.

MAKE IT A MARGS MENU

Variety is the spice of life, so why not offer three different flavours of margarita or a sampler board so customers can try all three. Frozen margaritas provide a neat addition to your list – just mix all the ingredients in a blender and whizz until smooth.

SHAKE IT UP

How about a margarita-making evening hosted by one of your staff? Guests could create a new recipe - special to them. You could include some history of the drink and offer a food and margarita bundle. Check out our margarita factsheet to help you with the background of this historically beloved cocktail.
