BAILEYS FLAT WHITE MARTINI

A classy cocktail with a rich, smooth flavour, frothy finish and coffee kick.

  • 50ml Baileys Original irish Cream

  • 25ml Smirnoff No.21 Vodka

  • 25ml Espresso

  • Coffee beans

  1. Fill a cocktail shaker with ice

  2. Pour 50ml Baileys Original Irish Cream*, 25ml Smirnoff No. 21 Vodka and 25ml espresso into the cocktail shaker and shake to mix

  3. Strain into a martini glass

  4. Garnish with three coffee beans floating in the centre of the drink and serve

    5. *Replace with Baileys Almande for a dairy-free alternative
