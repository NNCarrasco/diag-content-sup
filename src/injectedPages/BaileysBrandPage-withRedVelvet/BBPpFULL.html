CAN WE TEMPT YOU TO SOME EXTRA PROFIT?

There are three ways you can make Baileys part of the occasion and generate some tasty extra revenue. It’s all about timing and your team having the confidence to ask the question…

1. Over dessert

Offering a Baileys with dessert adds £2 per serve. Say: “The chocolate tart – nice choice! I know what goes really well with that. A cheeky Baileys on the side. Would you like one?”

2. in a hot drink

A shot of Baileys in coffee or hot chocolate adds £1 per serve. Say: “Treat yourself now you’re here – would you like me to add a Baileys shot to your coffee? It’s only £1 more…”

3. Post-meal standalone

A Baileys over ice at the end of a meal adds £3 per serve. Say: “Can I get you any teas or coffees – or how about a Baileys, it’s the perfect way to finish a meal…”

88% order dessert, 90% hot drink. Baileys can help inspire consumers to trade up.
