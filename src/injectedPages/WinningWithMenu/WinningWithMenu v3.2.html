WHY YOU NEED A drinks MENU… AND HOW TO MAKE A GREAT ONE

A brilliantly designed, well-executed drinks menu sets you apart, increases spend per head and reduces inefficiencies at the bar. The result? More sales in your till and profit in your pocket.

The art of great menu design in three simple steps

A well designed menu will inspire new choices, while allowing customers to make easier, more informed decisions. Here are three rules to getting it right.

1. Define your offer

You know what sells best, so focus on what your customers want. Creating signature serves from those drinks will make them unique to you. Remember to include special occasions and seasonal variations to keep your offer fresh.

2. Lay out the page

Less is more so de-clutter, grouping drinks into small, by-the-category sections. Use tempting imagery and descriptions, highlighting profitable serves with frames or colours. A head bartender’s recommendation can work wonders.

3. Reduce pain of paying

Simplify prices by removing currency symbols and round them down to the nearest amount. Anchoring the list with an expensive drink can influence what customers will pay for others. Randomising the order takes the emphasis off price as the reason to choose a drink.

Get the best results

Once your menu is up and running, these tips will help to make sure it delivers the results you’re hoping for….

  1. Train your team to make each drink perfectly
  2. Place menus where customers place their orders
  3. Give staff the knowledge to make recommendations
  4. Make sure drinks can be executed well at busy times

Always stock up with the best ingredients and brands

Make your offering stand out with the most recognisable brands at your bar. This lets customers know that they will get the very best version of their drink.
