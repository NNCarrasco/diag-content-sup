PUT SOME FIRE IN THEIR BELLIES
Ever wondered why Guinness pairs so well with grilled and barbecued dishes? The answer is in the barley. We roast it at a fiery 232°C, triggering a reaction that brings out its bold and distinctive taste. It's the same reaction that makes the taste of food made with fire so mouth-wateringly delicious. That's why our beers compliment flame-kissed food so well. Whether it's pan-seared, grilled, charred or smoked. Fire brings out the best in food. Especially when it's paired with Guinness.
