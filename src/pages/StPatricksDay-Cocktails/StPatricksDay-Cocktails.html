Bring a little irish charm to your venue with these St Patrick’s day cocktails

The theme is green with these easy-to-serve drinks, perfect for St Patrick’s Day celebrations…

One of the ways your venue can stand out for St Patrick’s Day is with the drinks you offer. Creating even just a small range of shamrock showpieces shows how committed you are to marking the occasion in style. Here are some suggestions…

St Patrick’s Day

Perfect for St. Patricks Day Parties! This green cocktail recipe is a breeze to make. Celebrate St. Paddy’s in style!

SHAMROCK SOUR

St Patrick’s Day

Not only does this cocktail sounds truly Irish, but it also is packed full of flavour!

  • 2 tablespoons lime juice

  • ½ tablespoon lemon juice

  • ¼ cup simple syrup

  • 25ml Roe & Co Irish Whiskey

  • 1 tiny drop of green food coloring

  • Lime wedges to garnish

  1. Combine ingredients and shake well

  2. Strain over ice

  3. Garnish with a lime wedge

MATCHA GINGER KOMBUCHA FIZZ

St Patrick’s Day

It’s not just super green in colour, it’s also filled with loads of super greens

  • 50 ml Smirnoff vodka

  • 2 teaspoons honey

  • 1/4 teaspoon matcha powder

  • 200ml ginger kombucha

  • 1 lime cut into wedges

  • A handful of fresh mint for garnish

  1. Add all ingredients into a shaker and shake for at least 30 seconds

  2. Fill two chilled glasses with ice and pour the matcha vodka over

  3. Squeeze in the juice from a lime wedge

  4. Fill the rest of the cup with ginger kombucha and garnish with a few fresh mint leaves

DID YOU KNOW…
A shamrock symbolises hope, love, and life. St. Patrick reportedly used shamrocks to explain the Holy Trinity, but later interpretations also said the three leaves are meant to symbolise hope, love, and faith. If there's a fourth leaf, it symbolises luck, which is why we consider four-leaf clovers to be lucky.
