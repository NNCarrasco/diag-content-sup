Key pairings

To get you started, here are a few beer and food pairing suggestions :

Light, refreshing pilsner

Taste: Clean, with a full bodied and rounded mouthfeel and a mild bitterness, complemented by subtle citrus notes and a crisp finish.

Pairing: Great for cutting through rich batters and sauces, making this the ideal accompaniment to fish and chips, pizza and BBQ-style dishes.
