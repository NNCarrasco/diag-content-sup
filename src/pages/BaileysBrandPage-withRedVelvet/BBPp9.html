RED VELVET CUPCAKE MARTINI

A perfect sweet treat

  • 4 oz Chocolate liqueur

  • 4 oz Vanilla vodka

  • 4 oz Baileys Red Velvet Cupcake

  • 1 Chocolate

  • Ice

  1. Pour the vodka, chocolate liqueur and Baileys into a shaker

  2. Add ice and shake

  3. Pour into two chocolate rimmed martini glasses

  4. Shave chocolate or red velvet crumbs into the top
