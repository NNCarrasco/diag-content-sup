Johnnie & elderflower

Inspired by London

Floral and delicious, the surprising and popular flavour of elderflower will appeal to younger drinkers.

Download recipe card

  • 50ml Johnnie Walker Black Label

  • 20ml elderflower cordial

  • 100ml chilled soda

  • Elderflower & lavender sprig garnish

  1. Elderflower & lavender sprig garnish

  2. Add the Johnnie Walker and elderflower cordial

  3. Top up with soda

  4. Garnish with a sprig of lavender and elderflower
