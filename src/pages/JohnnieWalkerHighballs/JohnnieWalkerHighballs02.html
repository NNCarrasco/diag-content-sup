johnnie & ginger

Inspired by Cape Town

Whisky and ginger are a match made in heaven, and even more so when you consider the smoky thread that runs through every expression of Johnnie Walker - this is a great spicy, fiery, citric flavour to add to your menu.

Download recipe card

  • 50ml Johnnie Walker Black Label

  • 150ml ginger ale

  • Ginger slice and star anise garnish

  1. Fill a highball glass with ice

  2. Add the Johnnie Walker and ginger ale

  3. Garnish with a ginger slice and star anise

Johnnie & peach

Inspired by New York

The classic American flavour, beloved in iced tea around the world! This visually appealing serve will add a touch of New York style to your drink selection.

Download recipe card

  • 50ml Johnnie Walker Black Label

  • 150ml ginger ale or sparkling peach iced tea

  • Peach fan & thyme sprig garnish

  1. Fill a highball glass with ice

  2. Add the Johnnie Walker and ginger ale or peach tea

  3. Garnish with a peach fan and thyme sprig
