Starter Suggestion

Salmon Gravadlax, with blinis and sour cream

A fresh, light starter to accompany your cocktails.

  • One cleaned fillet salmon, boned and skinned

  • 700g salt

  • 300g sugar

  • One large bunch dill

  • 100g sour cream

  • 30 blinis

  1. Place the salmon on a tray

  2. Mix the sugar, salt and half the dill and cover the salmon fillet

  3. Leave for 6 hours to cure, wash under cold water and pat dry

  4. Slice thinly to order and serve with blinis and some sour cream
