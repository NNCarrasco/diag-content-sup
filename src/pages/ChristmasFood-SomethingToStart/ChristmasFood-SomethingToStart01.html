Gordon’s Hot Pink

Cosy pubs

What’s hotter than Gordon’s Pink? Gordon’s Hot Pink. A winter warmer with spices, juniper notes and a fruity twist.

Drink Style

Sweet & Tasty

Season/Occasion

Winter | Festive

Alcohol content

1.9 units

Wow factor
Difficulty

  • 50ml Gordon’s Premium Pink gin

  • 150ml Cranberry Juice

  • 1 Cinnamon stick

  • 1 star anise

  • 2 cloves

  • 1 pinch ground ginger

  • 1 pinch grated nutmeg

  1. Add all ingredients to a small saucepan and gently heat through until piping hot

  2. Strain into a heat proof copa glass to remove the mulling spices

  3. Garnish with 2 fresh raspberries, a cinnamon stick and a star anise
