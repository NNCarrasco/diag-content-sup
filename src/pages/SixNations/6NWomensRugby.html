TAKE BOOKINGS

If you show women’s rugby, folks will make a night of it. Start with the women’s and they’ll stay for the men’s. Get some group bookings on the goeven if it’s just to reserve tables. You could go the whole way and offer pre-paid food and drinks packages –whatever works for you. Have a word with localwomen’s rugby clubs–‘we take bookings, ladies!’–and leave them some promotional flyers or leaflets with the fixtures you’ll be showing.
