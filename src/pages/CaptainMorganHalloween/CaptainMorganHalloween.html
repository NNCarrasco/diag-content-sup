Count Captain

A deliciously spooky blend of Captain Morgan, grenadine, and citrus juices.

Drink Style

Fruity & Fresh | Sweet & Tasty

Season/Occasion

Halloween

Alcohol content

2 units / 14g

Download recipe card

  • 50ml Captain Morgan (White rum or Original Spiced Gold)

  • 25ml grenadine

  • 25ml lemon juice

  • 12.5ml lime juice

  • 100ml orange juice

  • Glacier cherries

  1. Add the Captain Morgan (White Rum or Original Spiced Gold) lemon juice and grenadine to a tall glass

  2. Add ice to fill the glass and top with orange

  3. Gently stir for the grenadine to rise and cause a ‘bloody’ effect

  4. Garnish with x2 glacier cherries

Apple 'n' Ginger Captain

Dive into a delicious blend of Captain Morgan, ginger ale and a good slug of apple juice.

  • 50ml Baileys Original

  • 20ml espresso

  • 135ml milk

  • Cinnamon stick

  • Nutmeg

  • Whipped cream

  1. Pour 250ml of steaming milk into a disposable cup

  2. Add one espresso shot

  3. Pour in 50ml of Baileys

  4. Add nutmeg, whipped cream and cinnamon stick to serve
