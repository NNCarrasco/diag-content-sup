  • 35.5 ml Smirnoff

  • 150 ml soda

  • Half a citrus fruit

  1. Fill a highball glass with ice and add 35.5ml of Smirnoff

  2. Firmly squeeze the juice of half a citrus fruit into the glass

  3. Top up with soda water and garnish with wedges or wheels of the same fruit
