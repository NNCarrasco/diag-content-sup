Introducing Mark Moriarty

As Diageo’s Head Chef in Residence Mark uses his knowledge of spirits to bring their unique characteristics to life through food, creating uniquely immersive taste experiences.

An award-winning chef, Mark is also a business owner, his innovative pop-up restaurant concept taking him to some of the world’s most glamorous locations.

He was named in Forbes’ ‘30 Under 30’ for Innovation in the Arts in 2018, is a previous winner of the San Pellegrino World Young Chef of the Year, recently appeared on the BBC’s Saturday Kitchen and has just launched a new television series documenting his approach to cooking called ‘Beyond the Menu’. Mark’s talent and passion for food, flavour and storytelling, as well as his international accolades, make him the perfect person to step up to the plate as Diageo’s first ever Head Chef in Residence.
