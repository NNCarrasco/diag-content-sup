BAILEYS PUMPKIN SPICED LATTE

A delicious blend of baileys, coffee and unmistakably autumnal spices.

  • 50ml Baileys Pumpkin Spice

  • 250ml milk

  • 1 shot of espresso

  • Whipped cream, nutmeg, cinnamon stick

  1. Pour 250ml of steaming milk into a cup

  2. Add one espresso shot

  3. Pour in 50ml of Baileys Pumpkin Spice

  4. Add nutmeg, whipped cream and cinnamon stick to serve
