GUINNESS and ST PATRICK’S DAY

Through its authentic Irish heritage and values of togetherness and friendship, Guinness is synoynmous with St. Patrick’s Day. For many customers, it’s the chance to share great times with those closest to them and celebrate like no other time of year. You can play your part in making this year’s festivities their best yet, with a brilliant Guinness experience.

YOUR KEYS TO ST PATRICK’S SUCCESS

1. Focus on quality

For some, this will be their first taste of Guinness. Following the famous 7Cs on every serve will make sure they receive the best quality experience.

FIND OUT MORE

2. Dress to impress

Let your customers know well in advance that you’re gearing up for a St Patrick’s party to remember by dressing your venue with eye-catching POS. Venues using our St Patrick’s Day kits saw a 20% uplift in sales last year.*

*CGA data tp w/e 30/03/2019

3. Remember the food

A few subtle changes or the addition of a couple of simple dishes to your menu could greatly enhance the St Patrick’s experience for your customers.

Think about special offers, such as a lunch menus with a traditional Irish Stew or Boxty and Coddle. Offer them both with a pint of Guinness or one of our themed cocktails as a meal deal.

4. Bring the entertainment

In outlet entertainment and competitions can all help to bring customers to your door in greater numbers – especially if you promote them well in advance. With St Patrick’s falling on a Tuesday, the celebrations will start on the Friday before and continue them across the weekend.

This is also when the Six Nations rugby will be reaching its climax, with Ireland taking on France in Paris in a potentially title-deciding match.
