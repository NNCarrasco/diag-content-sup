Starter Suggestion

Baileys & Brownie

Chocoholics will love this rich chocolate brownie generously drizzled with delicious Baileys Original. The ultimate after-dinner treat!

  • 50ml Baileys Original Irish Cream Liqueur

  • One scoop good quality vanilla ice-cream

  • Fresh blueberries and raspberries

  • One chocolate brownie

  • Caramelised cocoa nibs

  1. Pour 50ml Baileys Irish Cream over the chocolate brownie and add scoop of ice cream

  2. Garnish with blueberries or raspberries and cocoa nibs to serve
