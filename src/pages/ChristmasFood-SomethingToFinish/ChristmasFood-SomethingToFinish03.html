Starter Suggestion

Fresh pineapple on ice with lime and salt

This light and sweet treat is perfect for serving alongside post-dinner drinks.

  • One large ripe pineapple

  • 300g crushed ice

  • Two limes

  • Maldon salt

  1. Peel and portion the pineapple into long fingers
    (approx 7x3cm)

  2. Season with some lime zest and salt and serve over ice
